About Our Retreats

Garrison Retreats are 4-day, resiliency-focused experiences that provide a unique opportunity for veterans to connect with peers in a safe and supportive environment. Through a combination of workshops, outdoor activities, and shared experiences, we aim to empower veterans on their journey to post-military life.
